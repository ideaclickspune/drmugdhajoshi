About Us

Why Should You Trust Us? Know About Dr. Mugdha!

Dr. Mugdha Joshi is an experienced Consultant Obstetrician and Gynecologist in Bavdhan, Pune, with over 17 years in women’s healthcare. She holds an MBBS and DNB in Obstetrics & Gynecology, and is registered with the Maharashtra Medical Council (Reg. No. 2005032045).

She provides comprehensive care in pregnancy management, infertility treatment, gynecological surgeries, and women’s wellness, combining medical expertise with a compassionate approach.

Dr. Joshi consults at Shri Waman Ganesh Clinic, Bavdhan, and is also associated with Chellaram Hospital, Pune for advanced care and surgeries.
